Unlike traditional 2D design tools, Xrchitect immerses users in a fully interactive, realistic 3D environment, allowing them to design and manipulate spaces within actual or virtual rooms. TeleTwin is an innovative Digital Twin solution that creates realistic 3D representations of telecom towers for infrastructure management and planning. By combining photorealistic modeling with real-time data integration, TeleTwin revolutionizes how telecom infrastructure is managed, planned, and maintained, reducing costs and improving network reliability.
